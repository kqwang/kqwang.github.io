Biography

I am Kaiqiang Wang, now a postdoctoral researcher in the Lab of Prof. Edmund Y. Lam at the University of Hong Kong (HKU). Before that, I did postdoctoral research in the Lab of Prof. Renjie Zhou at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I received my bachelor's degree and PhD in 2016 and 2022, respectively, at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), where he was advised by Prof. Jianlin Zhao.

I am an enthusiast for computational imaging and deep learning.
