Biography

I am Kaiqiang Wang, now a postdoctoral researcher in the Lab of Prof. Edmund Y. Lam at the University of Hong Kong (HKU). Before that, I did postdoctoral research in the Lab of Prof. Renjie Zhou at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I received my PhD at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU) under the supervision of Prof. Jianlin Zhao, Prof. Jianglei Di, and Prof. Kemao Qian.

I am an enthusiast for computational imaging and deep learning.
